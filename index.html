ABOUT IRONMAN

TONY STARK

Iron Man, created by Marvel Comics, is a genius inventor and billionaire named Tony Stark. Using his advanced technology, he builds a powered suit of armor to become a superhero. Known for his intelligence, charisma, and sacrifice, he plays a vital role in the Avengers, protecting the world from various threats.

Iron Man's legacy is defined not only by his technological brilliance but also by his courage and humanity. He faces personal struggles, including his own mortality, yet remains steadfast in his mission to protect Earth. His sacrifice in Avengers: Endgame epitomizes his journey from a self-centered billionaire to a selfless hero, solidifying him as a symbol of hope, innovation, and resilience for the Marvel Universe.
